# Exercise Library Reference

## Chest Exercises

### Bench Press
- **Category**: Chest
- **Equipment**: Barbell
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Pectoralis major, Triceps, Front delts
- **Rep Range**: 6-12
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Retract and depress shoulder blades
2. Arch upper back, not lower back
3. Feet flat on floor, drive through legs
4. Bar path: slight diagonal from chest to above shoulders
5. Touch chest at nipple line
6. Full lockout at top

**Variations**:
- Incline Bench Press (30-45Â°)
- Decline Bench Press
- Close Grip Bench Press
- Pause Bench Press

### Incline Dumbbell Press
- **Category**: Chest (upper)
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells, Incline bench
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Upper pectoralis, Triceps
- **Rep Range**: 8-12
- **Progression**: +2kg per dumbbell

**Form Cues**:
1. Bench at 30-45 degrees
2. Dumbbells at shoulder level to start
3. Press up and slightly together
4. Control the negative

---

### Cable Fly
- **Category**: Chest
- **Equipment**: Cable machine
- **Type**: Isolation
- **Primary Muscles**: Pectoralis major
- **Rep Range**: 10-15
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting 15 reps

**Form Cues**:
1. Slight forward lean
2. Soft bend in elbows throughout
3. Bring hands together at chest height
4. Squeeze at peak contraction

---

## Back Exercises

### Bent Over Barbell Row
- **Category**: Back
- **Equipment**: Barbell
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Lats, Rhomboids, Rear delts, Biceps
- **Rep Range**: 8-12
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Hip hinge, back at 45 degrees
2. Brace core, neutral spine
3. Pull to lower chest/upper abs
4. Squeeze shoulder blades together
5. Control the eccentric

---

### Lat Pulldown
- **Category**: Back
- **Equipment**: Cable machine
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Latissimus dorsi, Biceps
- **Rep Range**: 8-12
- **Progression**: +5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Grip slightly wider than shoulders
2. Lean back slightly
3. Pull to upper chest
4. Lead with elbows
5. Full stretch at top

---

### Face Pull
- **Category**: Back/Shoulders
- **Equipment**: Cable machine, Rope attachment
- **Type**: Isolation
- **Primary Muscles**: Rear delts, Rotator cuff, Upper back
- **Rep Range**: 12-20
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. High cable position
2. Pull to face level
3. External rotation at end
4. Pause and squeeze

---

## Shoulder Exercises

### Overhead Press
- **Category**: Shoulders
- **Equipment**: Barbell
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Front delts, Triceps, Upper chest
- **Rep Range**: 6-10
- **Progression**: +2.5kg (slow progression typical)

**Form Cues**:
1. Bar on front delts
2. Brace core, squeeze glutes
3. Press straight up
4. Head through at lockout
5. Bar path: around face, not forward

---

### Lateral Raise
- **Category**: Shoulders
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Type**: Isolation
- **Primary Muscles**: Medial deltoids
- **Rep Range**: 12-20
- **Progression**: +1kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Slight bend in elbows
2. Lead with elbows, not hands
3. Raise to shoulder height
4. Control the negative

---

## Leg Exercises

### Barbell Squat
- **Category**: Legs
- **Equipment**: Barbell, Squat rack
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings, Core
- **Rep Range**: 5-10
- **Progression**: +2.5-5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Bar on upper traps (high bar) or rear delts (low bar)
2. Feet shoulder width or slightly wider
3. Toes pointed slightly out
4. Brace core, take deep breath
5. Break at hips and knees simultaneously
6. Depth: hip crease below knee
7. Drive through whole foot

---

### Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
- **Category**: Legs
- **Equipment**: Barbell or Dumbbells
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower back
- **Rep Range**: 8-12
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Start standing, bar at hips
2. Soft bend in knees (don't change during lift)
3. Push hips back
4. Keep bar close to legs
5. Feel stretch in hamstrings
6. Drive hips forward to return

---

### Leg Press
- **Category**: Legs
- **Equipment**: Leg press machine
- **Type**: Compound
- **Primary Muscles**: Quads, Glutes
- **Rep Range**: 10-15
- **Progression**: +10kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Feet shoulder width on platform
2. Lower back stays on pad
3. Don't lock knees at top
4. Full range of motion

---

## Arm Exercises

### Barbell Curl
- **Category**: Arms
- **Equipment**: Barbell or EZ-bar
- **Type**: Isolation
- **Primary Muscles**: Biceps
- **Rep Range**: 8-12
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Elbows at sides
2. Full extension at bottom
3. Squeeze at top
4. No swinging

---

### Tricep Pushdown
- **Category**: Arms
- **Equipment**: Cable machine, Rope or bar
- **Type**: Isolation
- **Primary Muscles**: Triceps
- **Rep Range**: 10-15
- **Progression**: +2.5kg when hitting top of range

**Form Cues**:
1. Elbows pinned to sides
2. Press down until full extension
3. Spread rope at bottom (if using rope)
4. Control return

---
